Primary activity

MANUFACTURING OF SYNTHETIC MATERIAL AND MEDICAL CONSUMABLE MATERIAL, FOR ONE USE

• Cotton calico bandage, in all standard dimensions, manufactured by new technology (knitting) with great quality, equal distribution, proper form of the knit, simple for fast and easy application. The bandages are registered and comply to the standards of the European Pharmacopoeia number 5 (PH Eur 5).
• Elastic bandage, with its copyrighted commercial name ELASTICPLUS, is manufactured in all standard dimensions, with high quality, medium elasticity, pleasant and soft touch, equal porosity of the knit. Can be used multiple times.
• Non-sterile hydrophilic gauze swab and gauze swab products. The gauze swab is with high quality, density of 17 n cm 2 and is used for manufacturing of compresses in various dimensions and packaging, with or without RTG fiber.
• Sterile bandages, gauzes and gauze compresses by the copyrighted name LABINO. These products are manufactured in cooperation with the company SALVAMED AD Bulgaria, daughter company of the Italian leader for manufacturing of consumable material for one use, LUIGI SALVADORI, Spa Milano. The gauzes and compresses are manufactured in all dimensions, with or without RTG fiber, sterilized according to the E.O norms and comply to the European Pharmacopoeia's standards.
• Other products (accessories): First aid kit for cars, equipped by standards, first aid kit for companies, mini sets for motorcycles, terrain bags for first aid for different purposes and so on.

Secondary activities
